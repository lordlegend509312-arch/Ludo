📜 Royal Rules of Ludo
OBJECTIVE
Move all 4 pieces from your home base to the finish center.

STARTING OUT
Roll a 6 to bring a piece onto the board.

MOVING PIECES
Roll dice and tap a highlighted piece to move it forward.

CAPTURE!
Land on an enemy piece to send it back home. You get an extra roll!

SAFE SQUARES ★
Golden star squares are safe — no captures allowed there.

ROLLING 6
Roll a 6 to get an extra turn. Use it wisely!

WINNING
First to get all 4 pieces home is crowned the Ludo King! 👑
